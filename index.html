CS Kickstart

CS Kickstart 2017

Previous Next

CS KickStart is a week-long program introducing students to computer science while meeting other computer science students and professionals.


Students

This program primarily targets women who are interested in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and math.

Experience

Participants will get hands-on experience in programming introducing them to the creativity and diversity of computer science.

Exposure

Participants will also get the opportunity to visit a tech company in the Bay Area to see what life is like for computer scientists in industry.
